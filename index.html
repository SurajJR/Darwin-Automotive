ABOUT THE COMPANY



Why e-Bikes?

A hybrid-electric eBicycle aims to provide a viable, affordable and truly an environment-friendly replacement for fossil-fuel bikes. This first phase of this new revolution is achieved by modernizing bicycle travel. A mix of the old-school bicycle with modern day controllers and DC motor is guaranteed to make heads turn.

MARK 03

The MK03 is a pedal assisted hybrid electric bicycle, which means it can be driven by both pedalling and by the DC motor. This is an important feature which helps it differentiate from the existing ebikes.
